Why ExamRoom ?

We provide a web application to user through which examiners/admin can conduct online examinations and students can attempt those examinations. Admin can add examination on a date of their interest and enter an objective-type question-answers along with the correct answers. Students need to enroll and give the examination. Each question needs to be attempted in a maximum specific time-limit set by the examiner. After submitting, the students will be able to review their submissions and check the results.
